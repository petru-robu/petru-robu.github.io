Review

Cosmic Messenger este un album de fusion lansat de violonistul Jean Luc Ponty. Albumul este exceptional pentru vremea lui pentru ca imbina intr-un mod placut elementele de muzica electornica, sintetizatoarele, pedalele de efecte, etc. cu improvizatia jazz si muzica clasica. Vioara este un instrument neasteptat pentru genul asta de muzica. Un plus este dat si de modul in care e procesata vioara electrica, ce efecte are si ce ton se obtine.

Pentru mine, top 3 piese din album sunt urmatoarele:

  • The Art of Happiness - Pentru compozitie si structura.
  • "I Only Feel Good With You" - Pentru muzica, ambianta.

Despre Artist

Jean-Luc Ponty (born 29 September 1942) is a French jazz and jazz fusion violinist and composer. He is considered a pioneer of jazz-rock, particularly for his use of the electric violin starting in the 1970s.
In 2011, Ponty was invited by bandleader/keyboardist Chick Corea to join the group Return to Forever for a series of concerts throughout that year. The group was labeled 'Return to Forever IV', as it is the fourth incarnation of the group. Ponty had first recorded with Corea on his 1976 solo album My Spanish Heart.
